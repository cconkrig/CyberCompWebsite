Information We Collect

MQTT Communication Data

When you use the app to communicate with radio station MQTT servers, we may collect and store some or all of the transmitted data (for example, MQTT commands) for up to 90 days for performance monitoring and diagnostics.

Information You Provide

  • Radio station connection details (server addresses, ports, usernames, passwords)
  • Station call letters and frequency information
  • App settings and preferences

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and usage statistics
  • Network connection information

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the CARRELink service
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Diagnose and resolve technical issues
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Ensure secure and stable MQTT connections

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Connection credentials are encrypted using the Android Keystore.
  • Sensitive data is stored only on your device.
  • We do not have access to your encrypted local data.

Network Security

  • All sensitive data transmission is encrypted.
  • MQTT connections use secure protocols (TLS) when available.
  • We implement industry-standard security measures.

Data Retention

  • MQTT communication data: retained for up to 90 days for diagnostics.
  • Local app data: remains on your device until you uninstall the app.
  • You can delete all local data by uninstalling the app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. MQTT communication data collected for diagnostics remains internal to Cyber-Comp Technologies, LLC and is not shared externally.

Your Rights

  • Access the personal data stored locally on your device.
  • Delete app data at any time by uninstalling the app.
  • Contact us with privacy-related questions or concerns.

Additional Rights for Certain Jurisdictions

EU/EEA/UK (GDPR): You may have the right to request access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and to object to certain processing. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

California (CCPA/CPRA): You may have the right to know, correct, and delete certain information. We do not “sell” or “share” personal information as those terms are defined by California law.

Third-Party Services

CARRELink connects only to MQTT servers that you configure. We are not responsible for the privacy or security practices of third-party MQTT servers. Please review your MQTT provider’s privacy policy.

Children’s Privacy

CARRELink is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post updates within the app and update the “Last Updated” date above.

Legal Basis

This Privacy Policy is provided in compliance with applicable privacy laws and regulations, including but not limited to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).
